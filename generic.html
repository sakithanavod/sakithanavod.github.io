ABOUT

I am an experienced game developer with over 4+ years of experience in the industry. I specialize in game development and design, with a Bachelor of Science degree in Software Engineering. Throughout my career, I have worked on multiple large-scale projects, developing games for a variety of platforms.

In addition to my professional experience, I also have experience teaching game development-related subjects as a visiting lecturer for the University of Kelaniya for a duration of a semester.

My expertise lies in programming languages such as C++, C# and Blueprint visual scripting system (Unreal), and game engines such as Unreal Engine and Unity. I have a strong understanding of game development principles, including game mechanics, player experience, and game architecture.

My portfolio includes a range of projects, from mobile games to PC, showcasing my skills and expertise in game development. I have worked on a variety of genres, including action, adventure, simulation, and role playng games.

I am committed to delivering high-quality games that provide an immersive gaming experience for players. I am passionate about game development and am always eager to learn and explore new technologies and game development trends.

Thank you for considering my portfolio, and I look forward to discussing my work with you in more detail.
